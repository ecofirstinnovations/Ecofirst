ABOUT US

Eco-First Innovations is a trusted environmental consultancy firm dedicated to promoting sustainable development and environmental protection. We specialize in Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), Environmental Project Briefs (EPB), Environmental Impact Statements (EIS), and Environmental Audits. Our expertise also covers Air Quality Monitoring, Water Quality Assessments, and Soil Testing, ensuring industries operate responsibly while preserving natural ecosystems. In addition, we supply eco-friendly products that support businesses in adopting greener and more sustainable practices.

We also provide Regulatory and Licensing support to help clients achieve full compliance with Zambia’s environmental frameworks. This includes securing Hazardous Waste Licenses, Waste Management Licenses, Emissions Licenses, Pesticides & Toxic Substances Licenses, and Ozone-Depleting Substances Licenses. By combining technical expertise with innovative sustainability strategies, we empower businesses in Mining, Energy, Agriculture, and Construction to implement projects responsibly while safeguarding the environment for future generations.

Mission

To provide professional, trusted, and resultoriented environmental consultancy services that assist individual developers, companies, government agencies, and NGOs in adhering to environmental regulations when implementing developmental projects a business activities.

Vision

To guide clients to implement projects and Business activities without harming the Environment

Why Choose Us?

Eco-First Innovations Ltd is a leading Environmental Consultancy firm dedicated to maintaining high standards in Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment management. We are committed to adhering to international standards, including ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001, as well as meeting all legal obligations. Our integrated QHSE Policy is defined as follows:

  • Setting Clearly Defined Objectives.
  • Continual Improvement.
  • Competency and Performance Standards.
  • Customer Satisfaction.
  • Pollution Prevention and Resource Efficiency.
  • Injury and Ill Health Prevention.
  • Support and Champion Initiatives.
