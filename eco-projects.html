Harvest Service Station

This project involves the proposed construction of a fuel service station by the Harvest Group of Companies on New Ring Road, Kasupe Area, Lusaka District. Environmental Impact

Lotri Bay Expansion

This Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) addresses the proposed expansion of Lotri Bay Lodge and its conservation area by Namyst Limited in Siavonga District, Southern Province. The project emphasizes sustainable development and environmental preservation while expanding the lodge's facilities to enhance eco-tourism and conservation efforts in the region.

Siavonga.

Winners Chapel Church Auditorium

This project outlines the environmental considerations for the proposed construction of Winners Chapel International's church auditorium in Lusaka. Lusaka.
